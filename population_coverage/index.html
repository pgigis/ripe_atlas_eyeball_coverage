Eyeball networks coverage

An estimation of Internet users being covered by RIPE Atlas probes

The Eyeball networks coverage aims to measure the number of Internet users being covered by RIPE Atlas probes but also discover eyeball networks with few or zero probes. Eyeball network data are based on user population estimates provided by APNIC Labs.

The methodology can be found on the about page.
